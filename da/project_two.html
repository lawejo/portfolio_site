Creating an online presence for a start-up

In this assignment my group and I worked with a jewellry start-up called Ivaldesmykker - they had an existing homemade wordpress site, an empty instagram account and a facebook page that had just been initiated. There was no clear coherency in the way Ivaldesmykker expressed themselves online. So our task was to develop a Style & SoMe guide, a video(s) to be used in a fashion that would help this particular start-up promote themselves or their products. Finally we had to develop a new website and this is the part I played a role in.

Due to me being the one coding the website in this assignment, that is the focus I will have when taking you through this project. A bunch of code. If you want to check out specific fucntionalities that I've highlighted underneath here, click on the links to jump to those parts.

Coding mobile first

One of the requirements of this assignment was to code the website with the mobile-first approach and this entails using media queries with a min-width, since they are active with a screen of that has a certain minimum width, opposite of the max-width media-queries. On the right is a codepen of the media-queries used in this project.

Some of the advantages of the mobile-first approach is that a majority of users will visit your site on their phone and therefore you can argue that it makes sense to start your design and code there. Another advantage is that the page is loaded with much simpler code and the advanced styling will be added when needed through the media-queries.

See the Pen Media queries by axtecho (@axtecho) on CodePen.
